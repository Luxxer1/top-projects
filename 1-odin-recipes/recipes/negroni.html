Description

The negroni is a cocktail, made of equal parts gin, vermouth rosso (red, semi-sweet), and Campari, generally served on the rocks, and commonly garnished with an orange slice or orange peel. It is considered an ap√©ritif.

Ingredients

  • 30mL Gin
  • 30mL Sweet Red vermouth
  • 30mL Campari
  • 1 Orange Slice
  • Ice Cubes

Steps

  1. Place ice cubes in a whiskey glass and stir to cool the glass.
  2. Add the amounts of gin, vermouth and campari to the glass and mix them.
  3. Finish with an orange slice garnish.
